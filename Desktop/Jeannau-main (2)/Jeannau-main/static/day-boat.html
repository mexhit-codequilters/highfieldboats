A new vision of the premium Day Boat

  • Home
  • Motorboats
  • DB Yachts

Enjoy a feast for the senses and an inviting atmosphere aboard DB Yachts. These powerful Day Boats feature a sparkling design, the perfect setting for sharing fun and relaxing moments on the water. Sunny day trips with friends, moonlit summer nights, or high-speed cruises… aboard DB Yachts, every pleasure is intense.

Stylish Entertaining

Whether at the dock, on the deck, or inside the cabins, DB Yachts make a striking first impression with their streamlined design and incomparable elegance. Fine finishes and materials combine with high-end equipment for effortless, stylish entertaining. The owner's cabin and guest cabins are comfortable and bright. The bathroom features a surprising interior volume. On the exterior, the airy deck plan opens onto the sea, providing a dedicated space for entertaining, with: an attractive central island galley, an inviting saloon that converts easily into a sundeck, and above all, a sense of spaciousness, with two retractable side terraces providing direct swim access.

A multitude of posibilities await you.

Premium Day Boating

Aboard DB Yachts, you enter a universe of premium comfort: in the cabins, on the deck, and at the helm. With just a few accelerations, you can transport your guests to a sheltered cove or a beach with turquoise waters. At anchor, once the two side terraces are converted into swim platforms, you have only to choose your pleasure: diving, relaxing on the forward sundeck, or cooking while facing your guests, seated around the cockpit table. All the magic of the day boat is there for you to enjoy a unique experience at sea.

Premium Cruising

High-performance and secure, DB Yachts demonstrate perfection down to the smallest details. Ingenious design serves to enhance the quality of life on board. This is what makes a marvellous summer cruising boat, to enjoy as a couple or with friends. A fully equipped galley for preparing a tasty meal, a remarkably spacious deck, delicious evenings in the saloon, under a starry sky, a blissful night in spacious cabins, with their stunning interior design...

Ibiza Style

A Beach Club atmosphere aboard DB Yachts... Once the two side terraces are deployed, the deck transforms into a vast swim platform. The fully equipped galley features elegant bar styling, facing the luxurious and comfortable cockpit saloon. Music, refreshments, diving and swimming, a few dance steps, laughter… all the ele-ments combine for a fun, festive atmosphere. Just add a dream landscape and the sea!
